Water overcomes fire, fire overcomes gold, gold overcomes wood, wood overcomes earth, and earth overcomes water. Phasing is a process of dynamic equilibrium that ensures the diversity and harmony of all things

Color has a strange influence on humans, and in ancient China, the royal family placed particular importance on dress color. All the dynasties of China had their own dominant colors. Some dynasties had black dragon robes, some had royal colors, and some had red, so why did they choose so? What would be the significance of this choice? This involves the early Chinese, the so-called "five elements" theory , also known as the five elements of virtue theory. The five elements are divided into fire, earth, gold, water, and wood, and the phases are in this order, cycling through each other. Fire, earth, gold, water and wood correspond to: red, yellow, white, black and green. We can look at this relationship of restraint in the dynasties.

In addition to the complexity of the theoretical relationship, there are many practical factors. For example, some dynasties in order to illustrate the legitimacy of their own regimes, often think that the previous dynasty is legitimate, will recognize his regime; but often there are also many do not recognize the previous regime, resulting in the color is not complete in accordance with the theory of the five elements.
